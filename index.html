About Jeffery Phipps

Jeffery Phipps is a regional manager for the Microsoft TEALS program. It connects high schools teachers with computer science industry professionals. I have been with the program for the past four years.

Previously I was a teacher. I taught math and computer science. I worked in St Louis Public Schools. I enjoyed the work but was getting burnt out. I am grateful that the TEALS program found me.

In my sparetime, I am a stand up comedian. I have opened for many local comics and some out of town comics as well. I run a bi-monthly showcase in Belleville, Il. The next one will be August 31st.
